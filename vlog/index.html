About

Damani Marcano is a teenaged racing driver, racing among adults. At aged 16, after only two years in karting, he was promoted to senior car-racing in one step with Team HARD. Setting records in both the Volkswagen Racing Cup and the VAG Trophy Damani is now 17 and about to embark on his second season as a racing driver. Find out more...
